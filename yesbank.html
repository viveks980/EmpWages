How to configure this integration?
  • Enter the unque Customer ID of your corporate account after the bank enables API Banking for your account.
  • Enter the password provided by the bank.The bank will share the password in two halves,via email or SMS. If there are two admins for your corporate account, each admin will receive one half of the password.
  • Enter your live account balance with the exact decimal numbers as provided by YES Bank.Live Account Balance adds a layer of security while integrating your account.
  • Create a six-digit PIN to authorise all the transactions that will be initiated from Zoho Payroll.Your Bank will not provided this PIN. You will have to enter this PIN to initiate any tranction from Payroll.
  • Select the YES Bank account that you want to integrate.
  • Click Save to integrate your YES Bank account with Zoho Payroll.
